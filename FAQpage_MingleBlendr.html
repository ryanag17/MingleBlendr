What is MingleBlendr?
MingleBlendr is a social platform designed for you to meet new friends with similar hobbies and interests to yours. Meeting new friends can be difficult sometimes, and we want to make it easier for you!
How was MingleBlendr founded?
MingleBlendr came to fruition from the brains of Ryan, Alex, Claire, and Louis for their project in Web Technologies class in semester 3 of IBS.
How do I add my own event?
To add your own event, go to the events page, click on the "Create Event" button, fill out all required information, and it will show up on the calendar.
How do I message someone specific?
You can message people outside of the group(s) you are in by navigating to the "Inbox" page. There, you are able to message individual users or group chats, as well as create and delete new and existing groups respectively.
How do I create a group?
To create a group, go to the group page, click on the "Create Group" button, and fill out all information required to create your new group.
Can I deactivate my account?
Yes, you can deactivate your account by going to your account settings.
How do I change my profile picture?
To change your profile picture, navigate to your profile settings.
